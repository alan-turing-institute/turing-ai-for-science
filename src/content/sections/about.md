The Alan Turing Institute's Fundamental Research team invites you to speak at our AI for Science conference on March 31st, 2026, at the Royal Society, London. 

This one-day conference will bring together leading researchers and practitioners to explore how artificial intelligence is transforming scientific discovery and innovation. 

Topics will include: 
- AI for physical systems 
- Simulation-based science 
- Weather forecasting and climate modelling 
- Fusion energy
- Materials science 
- AI as a scientific collaborator and autonomous researcher 
- Trustworthy AI: safety, interpretability, and reliability 

Join us at this historic venue to share cutting-edge research, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and contribute to the UK's vision for AI-enabled scientific advancement. 

We would be delighted to have you join this important conversation shaping the future of AI for science. More details will follow soon.
